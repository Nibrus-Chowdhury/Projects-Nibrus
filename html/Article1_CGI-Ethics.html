CASE STUDY: The Ethical Challenges of CGI Actors in Films

What is being digitized? How is the information represented digitally?

What is the goal or purpose of digitizing this thing?

Scenes in movies are being digitized. These scenes would usually be recorded with the use of a camera, but with technological advancements and the use of CGI, many things are achievable such as bringing fictional worlds and dead actors back into scenes.

The purpose of digitizing this person is because movies need recurring characters but if the person is not alive then we either have to digitize them or add a brand new character out of nowhere. From this situation the film company is likely benefitting from digitizing their characters but the person who is dead might also benefit by being immortal in a sense.

What are the benefits and harms of digitizing this content?

Is our world better or worse because of digital representation?

The benefits of digitizing this content is the appeal it gives to a film's fanbase. The use of CGI can create beautiful scenes without having a stage setup to capture it on.

Article 1: The Ethical Challenges of CGI Actors in Films

Summary: CGI is, now more than ever, more prominently being used to digitally make dead actors come to life in film which has a dilemma. Does the actor consent to this use of their character and can we make that judgment without them? There have been many theories floating around but no decisive answer thus far. This could be used to fake videos much easier as well.

Article 2: Copyright Case of Tattoos in Games

Summary: NBA 2K made by the company Take-Two included tattoos in their game without explicitly permission by tattoo artists who claim it to be copyright. The defense for Take-Two is that their license from the NBA says that they can create avatars that are real to the players they represent and that technically includes the tattoos. The tattoo artist's claim is that the license does not explicitly allow for Take-Two to use their copyrighted art without any representation. Needless to say, Take-Two have won the case with their defense.

Article 3: Security Risks in Digitized DNA

Summary: There are many benefits to digitizing DNA. It can help medical professions and scientists to develop medicine, help people learn more about their family, help the police solve cases, and help with identification. However, there are also many disadvantages. The main disadvantage is that with easy access to information with minimal encryption/security, cybercriminals can hack and use this information for fraudulent purposes such as ransom, framing people in crimes, and tampering with evidence.

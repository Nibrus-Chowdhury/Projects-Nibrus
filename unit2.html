Lesson #3 The Need for Addressing

Describe how the Internet Protocol (IP) allows devices to easily connect and communicate on the Internet.

The Internet Protocol breaks down information into packages and connects to a wide network of different IPs which designate the destination and sender of the information which allows two devices with IPs to connect to this network and communicate through the information sent in packages.

Lesson #4 Routers and Redundancy

What are the benefits of building redundancy into a network? What are the potential issues with building redundancy?

The benefits of building redundancy into a network is that it makes for a fail-safe in case if one connection goes down then the network can still work. More connections means a higher demand which can slow down speeds or hurt overall efficiency for the cost of there being a fail-safe.
